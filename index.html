Hua Mulan

China's Legendary Heroine

Hua Mulan Profile from Ballad of Mulan (poem)

The story of Hua Mulan has been told thousounds of times through poems, songs, plays, movies, or simply just a story.Her story first came from a poem called "Ballad of Mulan". Her story is about how she disguised herself as a man to join the chinese army. Her reason to do such a thing was because she didnt want her fragile father to go to war. She stays in the army for about 12 years as a man. She went home after her service. When she came home she found her mother remarried since her father passed away. Then she was discovered as a woman. A man from the palace asked of her to be his mistress. She had no choice so she killed herself.
